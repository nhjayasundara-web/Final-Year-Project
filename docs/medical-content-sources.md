# Medical Content Source Plan

Use these as starting references for awareness content. Before production launch, all content should be reviewed and approved by qualified healthcare professionals in the target country.

- World Health Organization breast cancer fact sheet: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/breast-cancer
- CDC breast cancer symptoms page: https://www.cdc.gov/breast-cancer/symptoms/index.html
- American Cancer Society cancer screening guidelines: https://www.cancer.org/cancer/screening/american-cancer-society-guidelines-for-the-early-detection-of-cancer.html
- National Cancer Control Programme and Ministry of Health Sri Lanka reports should be added for local statistics and referral pathways.

Content review fields already included in seed articles:

- `lastReviewed`
- `sources`
- `category`
- `summary`

Recommended production fields:

- `reviewedBy`
- `reviewerRole`
- `countryApplicability`
- `version`
- `nextReviewDate`
- `clinicalDisclaimer`
